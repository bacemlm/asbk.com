About ASBK

Founded in 2005 in Beni Khedache, ASBK is a sports club dedicated to developing local talent and promoting sports among youth. The team currently competes in the Tunisian Championship, Level 3, striving to achieve excellence and foster a strong sports culture in the local community.

Our mission is to provide world-class training, foster talent development, and create opportunities for athletes of all levels to achieve their dreams.
